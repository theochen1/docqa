# Employee Handbook — Aldermere Systems

## PTO Policy

Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid time off per year. PTO is granted on a rolling basis
and unused days do not roll over past March 31.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

## Equipment

Each new hire receives a company laptop and a monitor. Replacement requests go through IT.
